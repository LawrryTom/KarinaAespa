Karina's Early Life

Karina, born Yu Ji-min on April 11, 2000, in Seongnam-si, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea, spent her formative years in a family that includes her parents and an older sister. She attended Seongnam Shingi Elementary School and Jeongja Middle School, where she actively participated in dance clubs, showcasing her early passion for performance. During her first year at Hansol High School, Karina was scouted by an SM Entertainment representative through social media. This opportunity led her to leave high school to focus on training, and she later obtained a GED equivalent certificate. She trained under SM Entertainment for four years, honing her skills in singing, dancing, and rapping.
